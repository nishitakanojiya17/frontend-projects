Background

Why Campus Mitra Was Built

In today's academic institutions, communication and information handling have become more complex than ever. Students, teachers, administrators, and parents depend on multiple separate platforms — WhatsApp groups, Excel sheets, notice boards, and outdated ERP systems — that are not connected, creating confusion and inefficiency.

Campus Mitra solves this by providing a centralized digital platform that manages all academic and administrative tasks in one place. It works as a smart link between students, faculty, parents, and administrators, promoting transparency and enhancing overall productivity.

Solution

How Campus Mitra Works

For Students

Check timetables, download study materials, monitor attendance, view announcements, and submit assignments — all in one place.

For Teachers

Upload notes and materials, mark digital attendance, post announcements, and track assignment submissions effortlessly.

For Parents

Access attendance records, academic reports, and performance indicators securely — stay informed without physical visits.

For Admins

Manage institution-wide data, create schedules, handle user accounts, maintain academic records, and oversee platform workflow.
